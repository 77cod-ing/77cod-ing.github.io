Accessibility & Best Practices

  • Ensure all product images have descriptive alt text for screen readers.
  • Provide keyboard-friendly navigation for category filters and shopping cart actions.
  • Support reduced-motion preferences for users sensitive to animations in banners or product sliders.
  • Use clear call-to-action buttons like Add to Cart or Buy Now with visible focus styles.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Overloading product pages with too many images or animations, which can slow down browsing.
  • Neglecting mobile responsiveness, leading to poor experience on smartphones or tablets.
  • Ignoring accessibility standards, which can exclude users with disabilities.
  • Failing to update inventory or pricing, which can frustrate customers and hurt trust.
